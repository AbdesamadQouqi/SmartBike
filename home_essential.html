Who are we ?

At SmartBike, we believe that sustainable mobility should be both innovative and accessible to everyone. Founded by a team of passionate EFREI engineers and urban explorers (Abdesamad, Yrvane, Jérémy & Steeve), our mission is to redefine the way people move through cities — combining performance, design, and eco-responsibility. Each of our electric bikes is designed in France with cutting-edge technology, premium materials, and a focus on comfort and safety. Whether for daily commuting or weekend adventures, SmartBike is more than a product — it's a smarter, greener way of life.
